What is TEDxGLAU

We present to you the fourth edition of TEDxGLAU with the aim of celebrating the idea of TED-'Ideas worth spreading' at the GLA University, Mathura.TEDxGLAU has been bringing "ideas worth spreading“ to GLA University since 2016. In these years, we have hosted TEDx on the theme, My Idea Lives Forever, The Idea That Stays and Breaking Our Own Barriers. Now, we are coming up with yet another amazing life-stories with Silent Leaders' Untold Truth on 10th November, 2019. On this day we will have some legendary guests and their heroic sagas to listen to. TEDxGLAU is all set to make an indelible impact on young minds. It shall be a room where energies will resonate, the fire of your dreams be rekindled and you will happen to meet the hidden legend inside you. Be there as words are less to define...



About TEDxGLAU

About TEDx

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TED Talks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection. These local, self-organized events are branded TEDx, where x = independently organized TED event. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events are self-organized.

our speakers

Shweta Kothari

Managing Editor at The Logical Indian

Shweta Kothari

One of the most noted anchor, Shweta Kothari, is currently the Managing Editor of Newsroom at The Logical Indian. Shweta started her career with BBC in Scotland after completing her masters degree in Journalism from the University of Sussex, UK. She continued climbing the ladder of success by working for Bloomberg TV India and NewsX. At NewsX, she served as a news anchor and senior correspondent. Not only this but there she also hosted the morning prime time show, '8 Straight' and launched their flagship business show, 'BusinessX'. With her fierce spirit and compelling aura, Shweta has been inspiring a generation of young journalists.

Vishal Bhardwaj

Data Scientist turned Astrologer

Vishal Bhardwaj

A perfect example of Defying Stereotypes, Vishal Bhardwaj is the World’s only Data Scientist Turned Vaastu Expert. Vishal is currently the co-founder of PredictionsForSuccess, a company with the aim to reengineer spirituality for the present generation. Pioneering his Data Scientist career with the software giants like Wipro, Mahindra and Fiserv, Vishal is redefining the rules of spirituality with his Techno-Vedic expertise. With a followership of more than 3,50,000+ and the establishment of a brand that speaks for itself, he is surely a leader to get inspired by. Vishal is an engineering graduate from Kurukshetra University and a Gold Medalist from Bhartiya Vidya Bhawan. While many data science aspirants study his technical guides published by none other than Oracle, he is influencing candidates to go where their hearts belong.

Sudhir Sharma

Founder & CEO, INDI

Sudhir Sharma

Sudhir Sharma is the founder and Chief Executive of INDI Design. He holds a post graduate degree from NID, Ahmedabad. He is known for his brand design work for many renowned Indian brands like Bajaj Auto, Bajaj, Finserv, Varroc, ICICI, Venkies, Standard Chartered Bank, Commonwealth Youth Games 2007, Pune City Branding and so on. Sudhir was awarded with the Most Promising Entrepreneur of the Year 2009 by APEA. He is the Publisher of the award winning POOL Magazine, India's only Design focused magazine. He hosts India's best yearly event on Design, "The Pool Show". He is a founder and President of the "Designindia"; an online community of professional Indian designers. He is a member of Indian Design Council, nominated by the government of India. He is Alumnus of the World Economic Forum (WEF) on the Global Council for Design. Sudhir Sharma, a man of excellence has definitely made significant contributions to India's design business industry.

Anant Khairnar

Cotton Sculptor

Anant Khairnar

Anant is a famous Indian sculpturist and artist who is mainly known for his unique sculptures made of cotton wool based on mythical gods and human expressions mostly. Anant has his name and sculptures registered in the Guiness Book of World Records, Limca Book of records. A Graduate in B.Sc. Chemistry from Pune University, Pune, he originally belongs to a small town named Jawhar in India. Despite working for 3 years in private sector and in government sector for 28 years, Anant was able to dedicate his thorough time to cotton sculpture as well. He has sculpted 2000 cotton sculptures setting records worldwide. His most famous displays of collection include sculpture of Mother Teresa at Guinness Book of World Records, London, Saint Namdev Maharaj sculpture at the residence of Smt. Pratibha Patil, Former President of India, sculpture of Lord Ganesha at the collection of cricketer Sachin Tendulkar. Taj Mahal in art gallery of Russia and many more all over the world.

Anveshi Jain

Actress

Anveshi Jain

Anveshi Jain is an Indian actress, known for her roles in online TV series like BOSS: Baap of Special Services (2019) and Gandii Baat Season 2 (2018). An Electrical Engineering graduate from RGPV Bhopal, she originally belongs to Khajuraho in Madhya Pradesh. Other than being an actress, she is also a singer, motivation speaker, dating coach and an event hostess (emcee). Anveshi is one of the most Googled celebrities in India in 2019. She is among the few Indian actresses who have 1 million plus followers on Instagram. She is the female lead in an under-production Gujarati movie (titled G), as well as in an upcoming Bollywood movie (titled Ghostana).

Naaz Joshi

India's First International Trans Beauty Queen

Naaz Joshi

Naaz Joshi, the India's First International Trans Beauty Queen, has set up a record of winning Miss World Diversity three times in a row, 2017-2018-2019. She is the winner of Miss Trans Queen India in the classic category 2018. She has been a cover model for six times and been a showstopper for more than 15 shows by now. She is an ex-model and theatre actress. Naaz is the world's only trans woman who has endorsed sanitary napkin pad. She has gone into rural India offering knowledge on menstrual hygiene and self-improvement. Working with multiple non-profits, she has raised funds for children with HIV-AIDS. However, she wants to expand the scope of her social activism. She is a trans activist and has been working on gender sensitization program at various schools and colleges. Naaz has dedicated her life to serving transgender community. She wants to make India trans phobic free and therefore demands equality for all the genders.

Pawani Khandelwal

Serial Entrepreneur & Social Activist

Pawani Khandelwal

Pawani Khandelwal is the founder of Aatm Nirbhar, a social venture. She is a serial and social entrepreneur who started her first non-profit to educate kids from the BoP when she was 13. Truly passionate about creating impact at the grassroot level in the Indian Society she started Aatm Nirbhar in 2017. Aatm Nirbhar is on the mission to build a nationwide woman-driven community that employs women from marginalised families to help up-skill other women. It is currently operating in the form of two-wheeler drivings schools enabled by female trainers in Mathura, Bharatpur and Jaipur. Pawani has studied social entrepreneurship at Indian School of Business, National University of Singapore and Tsinghua University, Beijing. She has been privileged to represent India at multiple social entrepreneurship programmes and conferences like World Economic Order, Start-UP Asia Berlin, Ashoka Changemakers, etc in different parts of the globe.

Poulomi Pavini Shukla

Social Activist

Poulomi Pavini Shukla

Poulomi Pavini Sharma is a lawyer, writer and humanitarian, who is profoundly committed to the cause of Orphans in India. She strenuously researched and wrote a book called, Weakest on Earth- Orphans of India which was published by Bloomsbury and endorsed by luminaries from all fields. Poulomi moved across 13 schools in 8 cities, and in each she worked with orphanages and Red Cross. She has filed a PIL in the Supreme Court on which notice was immediately issued. As soon as this PIL comes to it’s conclusion, one will realise that this will be one of the biggest changes in policy in human history as 20 million children will face a paradigm shift. She has worked with Mr Kapil Sibal, Justice JK Gupta (ex chief justice high court). The cause for which she has been working for was appreciated and discussed widely by leading news agencies and television channels.
